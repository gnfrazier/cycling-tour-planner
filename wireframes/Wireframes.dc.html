3bControl spec & states. Enlarged, with behavior and gestures.
On-map controls
Orientation
N
Tap → snap to north-up. Long-press → toggle heading-up (map rotates with you). Needle always shows N.
Zoom
+
−
Single tap, no confirm — repeat to keep zooming. Pinch also works; buttons stay for gloved / one-hand use.
Recenter / GPS
Filled = following your position (locked). Outline = map panned free; tap to snap back to you.
Layers
Opens the POI / parks / surface / construction toggles — no reload, updates live.
Contrast override
☀☾
Mobile defaults to Outdoor. Manual switch only for when the auto-read is wrong; synced per account.
Placement Stacked lower-right, in the single-thumb zone. ≥44px targets, 12px gaps to tolerate handlebar vibration and gloves.
